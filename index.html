The Kushan

From the burning sands of Kharak, the Kushan rose through exile, hardship, and war, forging a legacy of resilience and innovation. Now returned to Hiigara, their long-lost ancestral home, they stand at the dawn of a new era, determined to rebuild what was broken and reclaim the glory of the empire they once commanded. Their fleets are renowned for massive, heavily defended warships, while the great Kiith each devote themselves to mastering their own specialized fields.

In order to play this mod, you must:

  1. Own a copy of Freelancer. Make clean & vanilla installation.
  2. Install Freelancer HD mod using the clean & vanilla installation as a base.
  3. Copy your Freelancer HD installation somewhere else and copy Homeworld Rise of Hiigara on top of it. Overwriting everything if asked.

This mod is not standalone.
