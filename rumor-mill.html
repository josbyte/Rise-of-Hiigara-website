Rumor Guide

[ Lore_Filter: Online ]

What works well

  • Talk like a pilot, dockworker, trader, patrol officer, or station drunk.
  • Keep it punchy. One rumor should feel overheard, not narrated.
  • Hint at danger, shortages, patrols, wrecks, raids, politics, or strange signals.
  • Use places and factions that actually make sense in the mod universe.

Moderation notes

  • Not all rumors will appear, even if they fit the setting.
  • We may rewrite small parts to match in-game tone and formatting.
  • No hateful, sexual, illegal, or harassing content.
  • No personal attacks, links, slurs, or out-of-universe drama.

Submit Rumor

[Review_Queue]

Write it as if somebody at the bar has heard just enough to make the player curious. The best rumors feel half-true, slightly dangerous, and easy to imagine being repeated from table to table. Or just talk about their daily life, imagine yourself as entering the bar what would you hear. Keep Freelancer rumors in mind for inspiration.

Advice

- Do not write patch notes, full lore dumps, or mission text. A rumor should tease, not explain everything.

- Remember, current mod date is 11 After Hiigara Landing, The Beast happened in 15 AHL. So no events that happened in Cataclysm, yet.

- Mentioning other rumors, npcs, or events is encouraged to create a sense of continuity and immersion.
